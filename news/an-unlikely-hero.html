BREAKING: Blackthorn Staples Tyranitar and Gardevoir Released onto Free Agent Market

Pandemonium broke loose today at the end of Ice Tunnel. The news hit the papers late this afternoon, and concerned citizens of Blackthorn state:

"The sandstorm is over, the babes are gone."

"Quite frankly, I don't know what the Muk is going on anymore."

"If we don't have BABES on our team, how the Victreebell are we supposed to know which side of the field we are on?"

"Honestly, I'm just gonna miss Darude."

"They replaced our shining Goddess with a lot-lizard and a flower."

Blackthorn Babes (0-11) release the bombshell of the season with the news that their star Pokemon would be released into the free agent market on Tuesday 11/6. Tatsasuri, Dodonzo, Orthworm, and Salazzle are the Pokemon replacing the media-sensations: Tyranitar, Gardevoir, Lucario, and Garchomp.

Blackfang may have to fall back on its old roster, Houndoom, embodying the true spirit of Blackfang with his distinctive features. Additionally, Dondozo and Tatsugiri were signed as a package deal, known for their unbeatable chemistry in the Wolfy VGC Championship.

Gym Leader Logan, in an exclusive press release from PSPN states: "I can smell the fear ... Wins baby."

Chilling words, aimed directly at Levincia.

Levincia (8-3) however, is in a great position to retaliate, seeing as they have the ability to draft second, in response to this change in the roster. Articles soon to come on the draft response from Levincia, here at PSPN.
